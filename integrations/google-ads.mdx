To send offline conversions to Google Ads from LeadTrackr, you'll need to:

1. Connect your Google account
2. Select your Google Ads account
3. Create one or more **offline conversion actions** in Google Ads
4. Link those conversions to your **conversion labels** in LeadTrackr

## Step 1: Connect Your Google Account

Start by connecting your Google account inside LeadTrackr:

1. Go to your **Project Settings**
2. Click on **Manage Google Connection**
3. Click **Connect Google Account**
4. Follow the prompts to authorize your account

<Info>Make sure this Google account has access to your Google Ads account.</Info>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations/google-ads-1.png" alt="Connect Google account" />
</Frame>

## Step 2: Select Your Google Ads Account

Once connected, go to the **Google Ads Integration** tab.

1. Choose the Google Ads account you want to use

Using a Google MCC (manager) account? First select the MCC, then choose the sub-account you want to use.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations/google-ads-2.png" alt="Select Google Ads account" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Create an Offline Conversion Action in Google Ads

Next, set up a new conversion action inside your Google Ads account:

1. Open your **Google Ads account**
2. Navigate to: **Tools & Settings** → **Goals** → **Summary**
3. Click **+ New Conversion Action**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations/google-ads-3.png" alt="New conversion action" />
</Frame>

### Create a New Source

1. Select **Conversions offline**
2. Click **Add data source**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations/google-ads-4.png" alt="Select offline conversions" />
</Frame>

In the data source settings:

- Select **Skip this step and set up a data source later**
- Tick the checkbox under **Customer Data**

Then click **Save and continue**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations/google-ads-5.png" alt="Data source settings" />
</Frame>

### Define Your Conversion Name & Category

Now define the **conversion name** that will later be linked to a conversion label in LeadTrackr.

<Tip>
Need help choosing the right names and categories? Read: [Structure Your Conversion Labels for Better Ad Performance](/conversion-labels/structure-conversion-labels)
</Tip>

### Set Conversion Details

1. Choose a **conversion category** that matches the lead stage:
    - **Qualified Lead**
    - **Converted Lead**
    - Or any other category that fits your funnel

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations/google-ads-6.png" alt="Conversion category" />
</Frame>

2. Click **Add conversion actions**
3. Select **Connect data source later**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations/google-ads-7.png" alt="Add conversion actions" />
</Frame>

### Recommended Conversion Settings

If you're unsure what to choose, we recommend the following:

| Setting | Recommended value |
|---|---|
| **Value** | Use different values for each conversion |
| **Count** | One |
| **Click-through conversion window** | 90 days |
| **Engaged-view conversion window** | 3 days |
| **Attribution model** | Data-driven |

## What's Next?

Now that your offline conversions are set up in Google Ads, go back to LeadTrackr and set up your **conversion labels** to link your internal lead statuses to Google Ads conversion events.

<Card title="Set Up Conversion Labels" icon="tags" href="/conversion-labels/set-up-conversion-labels">
  Link lead statuses like "Quote Sent" or "Customer Signed" to Google Ads conversions
</Card>
