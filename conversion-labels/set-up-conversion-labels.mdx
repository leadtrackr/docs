## Step 1: Go to the Conversion Labels Tab

1. Open your project in LeadTrackr
2. Go to **Project Settings**
3. Go to the **Conversion Labels** tab in the menu

## Step 2: Create a New Conversion Label

Click **Create Label** and fill in the following:

- **Label name** — choose a clear name like "Qualified Lead", "Appointment", or "New Client"

- **Mark as Done**:
    - If enabled: lead goes to the *Rated* tab after processing
    - If disabled: lead can be qualified again later (e.g. for multi-step conversions)

- **Ad platform conversion** — link this label to the matching event in:
    - Google Ads (Create an Offline Conversion Action)
    - Meta Ads (using Event Name)
    - Google Analytics 4 (using GA4 event name)

## You're Ready to Track Real Conversions

Once your labels are set up and linked to the right integrations, you can start:

- Rating leads using these labels
- The real offline conversions will be sent back to your ad platforms
- And you can start optimizing campaigns based on **actual lead quality**
