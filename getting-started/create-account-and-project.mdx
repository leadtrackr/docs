## Step 1: Log in or Sign up

Go to [app.leadtrackr.io/login](https://app.leadtrackr.io/login).

- If you already have an account, you can log in directly.
- If you're new to LeadTrackr, just enter your email — your account will be created automatically.

## Step 2: Create a New Project

If you're logging in for the first time, you'll automatically be taken to the **Create New Project** screen.

If you already have access to one or more projects, click **Create New Project** from the project overview page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/create-project-1.png" alt="Create new project screen" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Fill in Project Details

Enter the following information:

- **Project name** (e.g. the company or client name)
- **Website URL** for this project

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/create-project-2.png" alt="Fill in project details" />
</Frame>

## Step 4: Choose a Pricing Plan

Next, choose the pricing plan that fits your needs.

Once you've connected a **payment method**, your project will be created, and you're ready to start tracking and rating leads!

## What's Next?

Now that your project is created, you can [add members to the project](/getting-started/add-members).
