---
title: "Add Members to a Project"
description: "Invite team members to your LeadTrackr project so they can help qualify leads and manage offline conversions."
---

## Step 1: Open the Project Settings

1. Go to the project where you want to add a member.
2. Click **Edit Project** in the top-right corner.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/add-members-1.png" alt="Edit project button" />
</Frame>

## Step 2: Add a Member

1. Navigate to the **Members** section in the sidebar.
2. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.
3. Click **Add**.

They'll receive an invitation to join the project and can immediately start collaborating once they accept.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/add-members-2.png" alt="Add member form" />
</Frame>

## That's It!

You've now successfully added a team member to your project.

Want to remove or change a member's access later? You can manage that anytime from the same **Members** section.
