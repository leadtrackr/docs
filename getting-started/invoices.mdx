---
title: "How to Find and Download Your Invoices"
description: "Need access to your invoices for accounting or administration? Follow the steps below to locate and download them."
---

## Step 1: Open the Project Settings

1. Go to the project where you want to access the billing information.
2. Click **Edit Project** in the top-right corner.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/add-members-1.png" alt="Edit project button" />
</Frame>

## Step 2: Go to Subscription & Billing

1. In the sidebar, navigate to **Subscription**.
2. Click **Manage Billing**.
3. You will be redirected to the billing portal.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/invoices-1.png" alt="Subscription settings" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: View and Download Your Invoices

Inside the billing portal, you'll find all invoices under **Invoice History**.

Here you can:

- View invoices
- Download PDFs
- Check payment status

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started/invoices-2.png" alt="Invoice history" />
</Frame>

## That's It!

You now have quick access to all your invoices.

Need to update your billing details? You can manage that in the same **Billing** section.
