## Inside a Project: The 3 Lead Tabs

When you open a project in LeadTrackr, you'll see three tabs:

- **Open** — New leads ready for qualification
- **Rated** — Leads you've qualified and marked as done
- **Pending** — Leads ready to be sent as offline conversions

## Step 1: Rate Your Leads (Open Tab)

In the **Open** tab, you'll see all incoming leads that are ready to be qualified.

1. Select a lead.
2. Rate the lead using the [conversion labels](/conversion-labels/set-up-conversion-labels) you've set up.
3. Repeat for all leads you want to process.

<Tip>Use clear and consistent labels, such as "Qualified Lead" or "New Customer", to track lead quality.</Tip>

## Step 2: Process Your Rated Leads

Once you've finished rating, click **Process Leads**. This will:

- Move the leads to the **Pending** tab
- Prepare them to be sent as offline conversions to connected platforms like Google Ads or Meta

## Step 3: Send Offline Conversions (Pending Tab)

Leads in the **Pending** tab are now going to be pushed to your ad platforms (this can take up to 1 day).

What happens next depends on your **conversion label settings**:

### Mark as Done = Enabled

- The lead moves to the **Rated** tab
- It won't appear in your Open tab again
- Offline conversion is sent just once

### Mark as Done = Disabled

- The lead goes back to the **Open** tab
- It keeps the status you gave it earlier
- You can re-qualify the lead again (e.g. for a follow-up conversion)

This is useful if a lead becomes a customer later on, and you want to send a second offline conversion.
